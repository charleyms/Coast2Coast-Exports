Coast 2 Coast Exports

Coast 2 Coast Exports is a Jamaican based export company, in the business of sourcing, packaging and shipping Jamaican products to the United States, Canada and the Caribbean. Items include ground provisions, herbs, spices, fresh fruits, vegetables and other authentic Jamaican canned and fresh produce.

Our products are packaged straight from the farm and meet local and international standards of food safety practices. Our swift shipping arrangements ensures products arrive fresh and on time for the consumer.
